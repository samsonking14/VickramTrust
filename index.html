our history

VICKRAM EDUCATIONAL AND CHARITABLE ENDOWMENT Trust is a non-governmental, non-profit making, non-sectarian, voluntary social service organization established in the year of 2017, under Indian Charitable Trusts Act 1882. The Governing body consists of 5 Trustees who are very committed and dedicated in community development and Nature Conservation. The Trust is founded by Vinoth Kumar.J, who is the Founder cum Author of the Trust.
‍
VICKRAM EDUCATIONAL AND CHARITABLE ENDOWMENT Trust was established by a group of like-minded individuals with good educational background and committed to social change with the vision of creating self-reliant communities and nature conservation for the benefit of present and future generations.
‍
VICKRAM EDUCATIONAL AND CHARITABLE ENDOWMENT Trust, Popularly Known as VECET Trust is a non-governmental, non-profit making voluntary social service organization established in 2017 under Indian Charitable Trusts Act of 1882. It is also registered as a charity under the Income Tax act 80G.

our mission

The Trust has a mission to enable disadvantaged groups through an integrated approach that addresses their physical, educational, environmental and economic welfare. The organization visualizes global transformation through local change.

our vision

VICKRAM EDUCATIONAL AND CHARITABLE ENDOWMENT Trust envisions enabling needy communities to become self-reliant through integrated approaches and to conserve natural resources for the benefit of present and future communities

What is the VECET?

The Vickram Educational and Charitable Endowment Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting education and various charitable activities. Our mission is to provide financial assistance, resources, and support to students, educational institutions, and community initiatives.

What are the main
objectives of the trust?
  • To provide scholarships and financial aid to deserving students.
  • To support educational institutions and infrastructure development.
  • To engage in charitable activities that benefit the underprivileged and marginalized communities.
  • To promote educational and vocational training programs.
How can I get involved
with the trust?

You can get involved by volunteering, donating, or participating in our events and initiatives. We are always looking for dedicated individuals who want to make a positive impact in the community.

trust for further queries?
